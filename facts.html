Facts About Smoothies

  • The origin of smoothies date back to the 1920s where a man named Julius Freed created a similar drink to help his stomach problems.
  • In the 1930s, smoothies started to gain traction due to the creation of the blender.
  • The smoothie industry is a 5 billion dollar business.
  • The largest smoothie created in the world was 1064.61 gallons.
  • Ingredients:
